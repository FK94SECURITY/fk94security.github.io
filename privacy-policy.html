Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

Our Commitment: As a privacy and security consulting firm, we practice what we preach. We collect minimal data and our free tools process everything locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

FK94 Security provides OPSEC and privacy consulting services. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at fk94security.com and our free tools.

2. Information We Collect

Contact Form Submissions: When you contact us through our website, we receive your email address and message content. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Consultation Requests: If you request our services, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information
  • Information relevant to your security consultation
  • Payment information (processed through secure third-party services)

Technical Data: Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may collect standard server logs including IP addresses and browser information. We do not have direct access to this data.

3. Our Free Tools — Privacy by Design

Our free security tools (Vigía Scanner, Footprint Analyzer, Policy Generator, etc.) are designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • 100% Client-Side Processing: All analysis happens in your browser
  • No Data Transmission: Your inputs are never sent to our servers
  • No Storage: We don't store any data you enter into our tools
  • No Tracking: Our tools don't track your usage or results

When you use tools like the Breach Checker, you may be redirected to third-party services (such as Have I Been Pwned) which have their own privacy policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Deliver security consulting and assessments
  • Send service-related communications
  • Improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted communications (HTTPS)
  • Secure handling of client data during engagements
  • Limited data retention — we only keep what's necessary
  • No unnecessary data collection

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • No tracking cookies: We don't use analytics or advertising cookies

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may link to or integrate with third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting
  • Cloudflare: DNS and security services
  • Have I Been Pwned: Breach checking (when you choose to use this feature)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any communications
  • Ask questions about our data practices

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Contact form messages are deleted after we've responded and closed the inquiry.

10. International Users

Our services are available internationally. By using our website, you consent to the transfer of information to the locations where our hosting providers operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, contact us at:
