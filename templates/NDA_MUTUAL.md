## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship regarding security assessment services ("Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, each Party may disclose certain confidential information to the other Party.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by one Party ("Disclosing Party") to the other Party ("Receiving Party"), whether orally, in writing, or in any other form, that:

a) Is designated as "confidential" or "proprietary"; or

b) Should reasonably be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

**Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:**

- Technical data, trade secrets, and know-how
- Security vulnerabilities and assessment findings
- Business information, plans, and strategies
- Customer and employee information
- Financial information
- Source code and documentation
- Network diagrams and system configurations
- Any other non-public information

---

## 3. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:

a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;

b) Was rightfully in the Receiving Party's possession before disclosure;

c) Is rightfully obtained by the Receiving Party from a third party without restriction;

d) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information;

e) Is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt notice to the Disclosing Party.

---

## 4. Obligations

The Receiving Party agrees to:

a) **Protect** the Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care;

b) **Not disclose** Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;

c) **Limit access** to Confidential Information to employees, contractors, or agents who need to know and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement;

d) **Not use** Confidential Information for any purpose other than the Purpose;

e) **Not copy** or reproduce Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose;

f) **Promptly notify** the Disclosing Party upon discovery of any unauthorized use or disclosure.

---

## 5. Security Assessment Specific Terms

Given the nature of security assessment services:

a) **Vulnerability Information:** Any vulnerabilities, security weaknesses, or findings discovered during assessments are considered Confidential Information of the highest sensitivity;

b) **Data Access:** Any data accessed during testing, whether intentionally or incidentally, shall be treated as Confidential Information;

c) **Tools and Methods:** FK94 Security's proprietary testing methodologies and tools are Confidential Information;

d) **Report Confidentiality:** Assessment reports and all related documentation shall remain confidential indefinitely unless both Parties agree otherwise in writing.

---

## 6. Term and Termination

a) **Term:** This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date and continues for [2] years, unless terminated earlier.

b) **Survival:** The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination and continue for [5] years from the date of disclosure, or indefinitely for trade secrets.

c) **Return of Information:** Upon termination or request, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify such destruction in writing.
